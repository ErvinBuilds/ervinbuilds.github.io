About me

More than just a developer.

Hey — I'm Antonio. I'm a Software Engineering student at East Carolina University with a passion for building things that actually matter. I started ErvinBuilds to turn my ideas into real projects and real income.

What makes me different is that I don't just code — I understand business, marketing, and design too. I can build your product AND help you sell it. That combination is rare, especially at my level.

I'm also autistic — specifically Asperger's and ADHD — and that's shaped how I think. I notice details others miss, I hyperfocus on problems until they're solved, and I build with real purpose. One of my projects, NEUVIA, is an autism awareness platform I built from lived experience.

I'm currently open to remote part-time roles, freelance projects, and internships that let me grow while I finish school.
